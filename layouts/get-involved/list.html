Build With Us

CSA Calgary depends on volunteers and contributors. You can help by attending events, proposing talks, joining the organizing team, supporting venue logistics, or connecting the chapter with local security leaders.

Volunteer

Join the organizing team to help run events, plan content, and support venue logistics. This is how the chapter stays running, and it's a great way to meet the Calgary security community up close.

Speak

Got expertise in cloud security, compliance, DevSecOps, or a related domain? Submit a talk or panel topic. We welcome practitioners at all levels. Beginner-friendly topics are especially valued.
