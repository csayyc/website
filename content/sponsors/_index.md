Sponsors help CSA Calgary provide accessible, high-quality programming for the local community.

Typical sponsorship opportunities include venue support, food and beverage, speaker travel, student access, and annual chapter support.

Recommended tiers:

- Platinum: annual strategic chapter support
- Gold: event series or major meetup support
- Silver: single-event or community support

Email [hello@csacalgary.org](mailto:hello@csacalgary.org) to discuss sponsorship.
