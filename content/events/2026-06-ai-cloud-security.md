Calgary's cloud security community is gathering for a deep-dive into how AI is reshaping the threat landscape - and what security practitioners need to know right now. Whether you're defending cloud workloads, advising leadership, or just staying sharp, this is the evening for you.

We'll cover how AI-powered attacks are evolving, practical defensive strategies, and the governance questions organizations can't afford to ignore. Expect a candid conversation between practitioners - no vendor pitches, no fluff.

**Who should attend:** Cloud security engineers, security architects, compliance leads, and anyone responsible for securing cloud workloads in Alberta.

**What to bring:** Your questions. Business cards are optional - good conversation isn't.
