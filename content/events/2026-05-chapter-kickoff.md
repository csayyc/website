Calgary's cloud security community is coming together for a night to kick off what promises to be a year of real, practitioner-focused events. This is your chance to shape what the CSA Calgary Chapter becomes.

Whether you're a seasoned cloud security architect, a security engineer just getting into the cloud, or someone who cares about building a stronger security community in Alberta - this is the event for you.

We'll cover the chapter's mission and operating model, discuss the Calgary cloud security landscape, and lay out how you can get involved as a volunteer, speaker, or sponsor. Expect honest conversation, no vendor pitches, and a first look at the 2026 event calendar.

**Who should attend:** Cloud security practitioners, security engineers, compliance leads, IT leadership, and anyone interested in building the local security community.

**What to bring:** Your ideas, your questions, and your enthusiasm for the community.
