CSA Calgary depends on volunteers and contributors. You can help by attending events, proposing talks, joining the organizing team, supporting venue logistics, or connecting the chapter with local security leaders.

Primary ways to participate:

- Attend upcoming chapter events
- Volunteer for event operations or content planning
- Submit a talk or panel topic
- Introduce potential sponsors or venue partners
- Share resources with the local community

Email [hello@csacalgary.org](mailto:hello@csacalgary.org) to start the conversation.
